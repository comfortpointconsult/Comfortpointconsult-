About Comfort Point Consult

Comfort Point Consult is an architecture and building consultancy firm based in Lagos, led by Babatunde Fasan. With over 17 years in the field, we deliver refined, functional and sustainable architectural solutions for residential, corporate and public projects. We design spaces that balance comfort with lasting style.
